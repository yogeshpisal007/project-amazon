AFCAT Study Material FAQs

Ques. Can I crack AFCAT with 3 months of preparation?

Ans. 3 months is more than enough to clear the AFCAT Exam. However, it requires a preparation strategy and sources.

Ques. Is English necessary for the AFCAT Exam?

Ans. Yes, English is indeed important when you are preparing for the AFCAT Exam. AFCAT Written Exam comprises of 30 questions on English.

Ques. How to prepare for the General Awareness Section of AFCAT?

Ans. General awareness is a very wide topic. The section contains questions on various topics such as science, history, geography, etc. Candidates can refer to the notes provided here to prepare for the General Awareness Part of the AFCAT Exam.

Ques. How many attempts are permitted in the AFCAT exam?

Ans. There is no limit on the number of attempts for the AFCAT Exam. The candidates can appear any number of times, given that they fulfil the eligibility and age criteria.

Ques. How to prepare for the AFCAT Numerical Ability Section?

Ans. Numerical Ability is one of the most scoring topics of the AFCAT Exam. Students should go through each topic in the Numerical Ability/Quant section and prepare and practice it well.
